SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great easy recipe for to make simple choclate cakes. It uses very common ingredients found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

Kitchen Equipment

To make this chocolate cake, you will need a variety of cooking equipment. You will need a mixer, spatula, scale, pan and a mixing bowl. These will be used throughout the directions

Ingredients

For this chocolate cake, you will need flower, butter, eggs, suger, and melted chocolate. For a reference of how much you need of each, refer to the image below.

Mixing

The first thing to do for the chocolate cake is to mix all the ingredients together using the mixer. I shouldn't matter which order they go in because they all will be mixed together at one point

Baking

After mixing the ingredients of the cake together, bake the cake in the oven for as long as you want. I don't know how long you bake for, so I suggest checking it every 10 minutes.
